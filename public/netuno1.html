The Best Cat In The World

Reasons Why My Cat Is The Best.

He is very affectionate

My baby boy loves cuddling, and whenever he's next to me (and awake) he demands to be petted. If I stop, he'll tap me with his cute little paw until I start again.

He hates everyone but me

Very loyal, yep. I'm the only one he'll sleep with, and the only one he allows to do things such as:

  • Give him medicine
  • Give him a bath
  • Brush his fur or cut his nails

He is super cute

This one speaks for itself. He's already cute just for being a cat, but being him, he's super cute.
